## 📊 AI Job Market Analysis
## 🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes AI job market trends to understand demand for job roles, required skills, salary patterns, and remote work opportunities. The analysis helps identify key insights for job seekers and businesses in the AI industry.

## 🎯 Objectives

-Identify the most in-demand AI roles
-Analyze salary distribution by job title
-Understand remote vs hybrid vs onsite job trends
-Discover top skills required in AI jobs
-Explore industry hiring trends

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
 
-Python
-Pandas
-Matplotlib
-Seaborn
-Jupyter Notebook

## 📊 Key Insights

-Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer are among the most in-demand roles.
-Python, SQL, and Machine Learning are the most frequently required skills.
-Many AI roles offer remote or hybrid work options.
-The technology industry shows the highest demand for AI professionals.
